Shahi Paneer

Shahi Paneer is a rich and creamy Indian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a flavorful gravy of nuts, spices, and cream. It's a popular dish in North Indian cuisine and is often served with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • 200 gm paneer
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1/4 cup cashew nuts
  • 1/4 cup almonds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps to shahi paneer

  1. Soak cashew nuts and almonds in warm water for 15-20 minutes. Drain and blend them into a smooth paste.
  2. In a pan, heat oil or ghee. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and saut√© for a minute.
  4. Add the blended nut paste and cook for 2-3 minutes until the oil separates.
  5. Add yogurt, cream, turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add paneer cubes and water. Cook for 5-7 minutes on low heat.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with naan or rice.
  9. Enjoy your delicious Shahi Paneer!
  10. Note: You can adjust the spice levels according to your taste.
